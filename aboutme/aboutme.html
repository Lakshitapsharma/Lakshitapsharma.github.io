I am Lakshita,
A UI/UX Designer from New Delhi, India.


I have always been passionate about the creativity around me, which makes me more curious about how I can apply creative ideology in the digital world. That is how my journey started from being a software developer to a UI/UX designer. My journey began with a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering. During my time in college, I developed skills in coding in different programming languages, but it never felt like the path I was truly passionate about. During that time, I also encountered a career gap.



In 2019, I restarted my career and education and landed my first job as a software developer in a small company, which provided me with ample opportunities to learn various aspects of the job. It was there that I developed an interest in designing interfaces, realizing that a product's purpose is not solely functional but also involves capturing user attention and enhancing their experience. This led me to delve into UI/UX design, leveraging my background as a software developer to better understand the workings of interfaces. This, in turn, facilitated effective communication with developers and product managers to conceptualize and create products.
